# Swiggy Data Analysis Project – Content

## 1. Project Title

**Exploratory Data Analysis of Swiggy Restaurant Dataset**

---

# 2. Project Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a Swiggy restaurant dataset to understand restaurant trends, pricing patterns, ratings distribution, and customer demand across different locations.

The goal of this analysis is to extract meaningful insights from the data using **Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn**. The analysis helps identify popular restaurants, high-demand locations, and pricing patterns which influence customer ordering behavior.

This project demonstrates how data analysis can help food delivery platforms improve business strategies, restaurant visibility, and customer satisfaction.

---

# 3. Objectives of the Project

* Understand the **distribution of restaurant ratings**
* Identify **top locations with the highest number of restaurants**
* Analyze **price distribution across restaurants**
* Discover **popular cuisines and food categories**
* Identify **high-demand restaurant areas**
* Extract insights that could help improve **business decisions**

---

# 4. Dataset Description

The dataset contains information about restaurants listed on the Swiggy platform.

Important columns in the dataset include:

* **Restaurant Name** – Name of the restaurant
* **Location** – Area where the restaurant operates
* **Cuisine** – Type of food served
* **Rating** – Customer rating given to the restaurant
* **Price (INR)** – Average cost for food

This dataset helps analyze restaurant popularity, pricing strategies, and customer preferences.

---

# 5. Data Cleaning Steps

Before analysis, the following preprocessing steps were performed:

* Loaded the dataset using **Pandas**
* Checked dataset shape and structure
* Inspected missing values
* Cleaned inconsistent or null data
* Verified data types of columns
* Prepared the dataset for visualization

Data cleaning ensures accurate and reliable analysis.

# 6. Exploratory Data Analysis

Several visualizations were created to explore the dataset.

### Rating Distribution

A histogram was used to understand how restaurant ratings are distributed.

Insight:
Most restaurants have ratings between **3.5 and 4.5**, indicating generally good customer satisfaction.

---

### Top Restaurant Locations

A bar chart was used to identify locations with the highest number of restaurants.

Insight:
Some locations dominate the dataset, indicating **high food demand in those areas**.

---

### Price Distribution

Price analysis helps understand the affordability of restaurants.

Insight:
Most restaurants fall in the **moderate price range**, making food delivery accessible to a wide audience.

---

### Cuisine Analysis

Cuisine distribution shows the most common food types offered by restaurants.

Insight:
Certain cuisines appear more frequently, reflecting **customer preferences and demand trends**.

---

# 7. Key Insights from the Analysis

### Insight 1

Most restaurants on the platform maintain **ratings above 3.5**, showing strong customer satisfaction.

### Insight 2

A few locations contain the **highest concentration of restaurants**, indicating major food delivery hubs.

### Insight 3

The majority of restaurants offer **mid-range pricing**, making food affordable for most users.

### Insight 4

Popular cuisines dominate the platform, showing **clear customer food preferences**.

### Insight 5

Higher-rated restaurants tend to receive **more customer attention and visibility**.
